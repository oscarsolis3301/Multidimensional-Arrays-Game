# Tic Tac Toe
This is a tic tac toe game made while practicing multidimensional arrays! It is very versatile and you can play against friends or against your computer. It also refreshes the screen whenever something major happens. It will display the board throughout the entire game so you can see what is happening. The great thing about it is that you can play against friends and it will detect who wins, loses, or if the game is a tie!

# Two Players
You can get your friends to play against you and see who is better at tic tac toe

# One Player
You can play against a bot!
